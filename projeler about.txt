visagerer:
"Faces lie, but masks reveal the truth. In this game, you play as a Mask Maker who must read between the lines. Customers will come to your shop with specific requests, but their words don't always match their feelings. Your job is to analyze their dialogue, deduce their true emotions (or the disguise they need), and craft the perfect mask for them."

How to Play:

Listen: Read the customer's request carefully. Pay attention to the hidden meaning behind their words.

Deduce: Decide what kind of mask they actually need (e.g., if they are sad but want to hide it, or a killer trying to look innocent).

Craft: Select the correct Eyes, Mouth, and Nose to build the mask.

Serve: Submit the mask and see if you satisfied the customer!


Features:

Psychological deduction mechanics.

Different difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard).

Unique characters with hidden agendas.



Controls: Mouse only
